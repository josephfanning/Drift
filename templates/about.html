About Drift

Drift is a simple, advertisement free social media web app built upon the python flask framework that allows you to share your thoughts with other users.


Drift has the following features

  • A secure login system utilising BCrypt to encrypt passwords
  • The ability to view other account's posts
  • The ability to logout from your account utilising session management
  • The ability to delete your account and selected posts associated with your account
  • The ability to add friends, view accounts' friends and posts
